KPK Province

Khyber Pakhtunkhwa is a province of breathtaking mountains, lush valleys, and rich traditions. It is known for its hospitality, bravery, and strong Pashtun cultural values. Tourist spots like Swat, Chitral, and Naran attract visitors from around the world. Peshawar, the capital, is one of the oldest cities in South Asia with deep historical roots. KPK beautifully blends natural beauty with cultural heritage and resilience.
